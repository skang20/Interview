Why do you want to design?

I love creating. Design gives me excitement. Everytime I design something whether a poster, an album cover or a painting, I feel accompished. Art is fun and beautiful. Without art, the world is such a boring place so I want to contribute beautiful creations for it. I studied bussiness in university but found it’s tedious. That’s why I changed my major, and so far I think I made the right choice.

What is diffcult or frustrating for you within design and how do you work to push past that?

First, the most diffcult is my lack of drawing and adobe programs skills. As I said, I don’t have much experience with graphic design so I find it pretty hard to hand draw and use design programs like photoshop and illustrator. So to push past that, I try to pratice whenever I can and I think I’m getting better. Second, the lack of inspiration sometimes. I conquer that I go online to look at other peope’s works and that helps a lot.

What do you see yourself doing after you fnish this program?

Anything. I would love to accept anything that is given to me. As someone who just graduate, I don’t think I should be picky. I need experiences in the real world and maybe after that I can decide what I would like to do as a profession.

What excites you about design?

What I love about design is the endless possibility. There’s so many dierent things you can create. Everyday, there are new designs that are made by so many talented people.
